About the Club

Club CoasterFusion was established in November of 2009 with a few key priorities in mind. The first and most important priority is you! We want to make everyone feel welcome when they register and become part of Club CoasterFusion. Whether your a seasoned roller coaster veteran or a newcomer to the scene, everyone is equal! Our number two priority is of course the price one pays to become part of our club! We want to make sure you get the best out of your membership, so why charge astronomical membership fees. Why not save some money!?

Membership Cost

Club CoasterFusion is the world's cheapest roller coaster enthusiast organization. We offer three different types of memberships that can be purchased by anyone from anywhere on the planet:

United States Memberships: $20.00 (Plus $5 each additional member, up to 4 allowed)

Canada and Mexico Memberships: $25.00 (Plus $5 each additional member, up to 4 allowed)

International Memberships: $30.00 (Plus $5 each additional member, up to 4 allowed)

When making your purchase you will have the option to add up to four additional members. You will act as the primary membership holder. Therefore all important club information and etc will be transmitted through you.
